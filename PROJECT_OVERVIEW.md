# AI Placement Copilot

## Overview

AI Placement Copilot is an AI-powered career preparation and placement assistance platform designed to help students and fresh graduates become placement-ready.

The platform combines resume analysis, job recommendations, AI interviews, coding assessments, skill gap analysis, personalized learning roadmaps, and application tracking into a single intelligent ecosystem.

Unlike traditional job portals, AI Placement Copilot does not only recommend jobs. It analyzes the user's profile, education, skills, career goals, interview performance, and coding abilities to provide personalized career guidance and placement support.

---

# Vision

To create an AI-powered career assistant that guides students throughout their complete placement journey from resume preparation to successful job placement.

---

# Problem Statement

Students often use multiple platforms for:

* Resume Building
* ATS Analysis
* Job Searching
* Mock Interviews
* Coding Practice
* Skill Development
* Application Tracking

This fragmented experience makes placement preparation inefficient.

AI Placement Copilot solves this problem by bringing all placement-related activities into one platform.

# Core Features

## 1. ATS Resume Analyzer

Analyze uploaded resumes and generate:

* ATS Score
* Skill Extraction
* Keyword Analysis
* Resume Improvement Suggestions

---

## 2. AI Job Search

Automatically fetch jobs from online sources.

Features:

* Job Search
* Smart Filters
* Latest Jobs
* Trending Jobs
* Recommended Jobs

---

## 3. AI Job Recommendation Engine

Recommendations are generated using:

* Resume Skills
* Education Details
* Career Preferences
* Coding Scores
* Interview Scores
* Location Preferences

Each job receives a match percentage.

---

## 4. Job Details Dashboard

Instead of redirecting users immediately to external websites, the platform displays:

* Complete Job Description
* Company Information
* Salary Details
* Requirements
* Responsibilities
* Match Analysis

---

## 5. AI Resume Generator

Features:

* Keyword Optimization
* Skills Reordering
* Resume Enhancement
* PDF Export

## 6. AI Cover Letter Generator
